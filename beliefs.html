Beliefs

  • Progress is good and we should actively work to accelerate it
  • Scientific and technological advancement are the main drivers of human flourishing
  • Most problems can be solved with the right incentive structures
  • We should take more risks and be more ambitious as a society
  • Decentralization and competition between systems leads to better outcomes
  • The scientific method and rationality are our best tools for understanding reality
  • We should be long-term optimistic but short-term realistic
  • Individual agency and personal responsibility matter a lot
  • Most institutions are failing us and need to be rebuilt
  • Cities should be denser and more walkable
  • We need to make it easier to build things in the physical world
  • Space exploration and becoming multi-planetary is important for humanity
  • Effective altruism has good principles but often poor execution
  • Markets are generally the best way to allocate resources
  • We should embrace nuclear power
  • Education should be more focused on practical skills and critical thinking
  • Most credentials are increasingly worthless - judge people by what they can do
  • Remote work and digital nomadism will continue to grow
  • Cryptocurrency enables new forms of human coordination
  • AGI will fundamentally change society within our lifetimes
